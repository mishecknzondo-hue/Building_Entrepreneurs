About Us

Building Entrepreneurs is a youth-led community-based organization established in 2023 in Malawi. The organization was formed to promote entrepreneurial thinking, innovation, and practical business skills among young people, particularly at the community level.

Guided by a formal constitution, Building Entrepreneurs exists to support youth in identifying, developing, and implementing viable business ideas that contribute to sustainable livelihoods, economic growth, and community development.

Our Mission

To promote innovative and sustainable business ideas among youth in Chikwawa & Nsanje Districts and Malawi as a whole, empowering them with entrepreneurial knowledge, skills, and mentorship for economic self-reliance.

Our Objectives

  • To nurture entrepreneurial and innovative thinking among young people.
  • To support youth in developing viable business ideas and enterprises.
  • To provide mentorship, training, and guidance in business planning and management.
  • To promote youth participation in local economic development.
  • To contribute to poverty reduction through sustainable entrepreneurship.
