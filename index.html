About Building Entrepreneurs

Building Entrepreneurs is a youth-driven initiative focused on promoting entrepreneurship, creativity, and sustainable economic development. We support young people by equipping them with innovative business skills and practical knowledge to create employment and transform communities.

The organization currently operates as a community-based initiative and is in the process of formal registration.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To promote innovative business ideas among youth in Chikwawa District and Malawi as a whole.

Our Vision

A generation of empowered, self-reliant, and innovative young entrepreneurs driving Malawi’s economic growth.

Core Values

  • Innovation
  • Youth Empowerment
  • Integrity
  • Community Development
