About Us

Building Entrepreneurs is a youth-led community-based organization established in 2023 in Malawi. The organization was formed to promote entrepreneurial thinking, innovation, and practical business skills among young people, particularly at the community level.

Guided by a formal constitution, Building Entrepreneurs exists to support youth in identifying, developing, and implementing viable business ideas that contribute to sustainable livelihoods, economic growth, and community development.

Our Core Values

  • Inclusivity: We believe entrepreneurship is for everyone, regardless of background or socioeconomic status.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity, experimentation, and responsible risk-taking to drive business growth and community development.
  • Collaboration: We foster partnerships with local organizations, institutions, and stakeholders to share resources and expertise.
  • Accountability: We are committed to transparency, responsible leadership, and continuous improvement.
  • Sustainability: We promote environmentally and socially responsible practices for long-term prosperity along the Lower Shire East Bank.

Our Vision

To become a world-class and profitable organization that empowers young entrepreneurs to build successful and impactful businesses.

Our Mission

To foster a strong culture of entrepreneurship among young people in the Lower Shire, across Southern Malawi, throughout the country, and beyond by equipping them with the skills, knowledge, and resources necessary to succeed in business and contribute positively to their communities.

Our Objectives

  • To nurture entrepreneurial and innovative thinking among young people.
  • To support youth in developing viable business ideas and enterprises.
  • To provide mentorship, training, and guidance in business planning and management.
  • To promote youth participation in local economic development.
  • To contribute to poverty reduction through sustainable entrepreneurship.
