About Us

Building Entrepreneurs is a youth-focused initiative based in Chikwawa District, Malawi. The organization is currently pending registration and aims to nurture entrepreneurial thinking, innovation, and practical business skills among young people.

Our Mission

To promote innovative business ideas among youth in Chikwawa District and Malawi as a whole, empowering them to create sustainable enterprises and improve livelihoods.
